Coffee Shop

What’s better than a fresh cup of coffee?

We offer the highest quality of organic coffee. The most incredible experience for those who appreciate the natural taste and aroma like us is our selection of around 100 varieties on tap with more to come. We are able to do this because all of ours are sourced directly by a small business Dear Green coffee. Our artisan coffees have always been handcrafted daily using only the finest ingredients without any added chemicals or fillers! This makes them healthier than even your typical specialty espresso, making it ideal for every day! Come to visit our coffee shop here and you will notice that it is filled with a wide variety of beverages. Here we offer both espresso and teas which are all very tasty! The cafe has an excellent selection at the bottom right corner as well as many choices for tea.
