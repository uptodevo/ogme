Gardening Course

For beginners and advance

Book your course now!

We welcome everyone interested in the gardening course from beginners to advanced. We will be able put you right with these amazing projects! The morning is a classroom lecture, and the afternoon is a practical demonstration within our garden plot to allow you a “hands-on” experience. We will cover all aspects of what you should be planting, soil conditions, and seeds so that you understand the life cycle and learn to grow your own success. Dress for the weather on the day but please wear stout boots or “wellies” and a packed lunch.
